INGRIDIENTS:
  • milk
  • nutella
  • salt
  • Oatmeal
  • Brown sugar
  • any berry

Step by step coocking recipe

Step 1

Pour milk into a small saucepan, put on fire and bring to a boil.

step 2

Reduce heat to medium-high and add oatmeal and salt, stir to combine.

step 3

Continue stirring, lifting the pot off the burner and returning it to the burner so that the mixture
does not boil until the oatmeal has absorbed most of the milk.

step 4

Remove from heat and add sugar and nutella.

step 5

Divide among bowls and place any berry slices on top.
